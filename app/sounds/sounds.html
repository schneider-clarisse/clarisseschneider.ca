Music has always been a part of my life. At the age of 4, my mother introduced me to the violin, and I was hooked on the vocal quality of its sound.

I loved the precision, the math, the beauty, the art; I was finally able to create something. I went on to perform with several orchestras, a privilege I will be forever thankful for.

Later, I learned about the power of an orchestra and the thrill of playing with others. Some of the happiest moments of my life were playing in the Toronto Symphony Youth Orchestra.

I joined a small band for two years, developing my improvisation skills and entering the world of alternative music.

Lately, I've discovered the joy of simply mixing voice with ukelele. I'm no singer, but I enjoy playing around and making people smile.

I hope you like my sounds, because that's the idea; music was made to share.
